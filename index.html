Here's the Success story of Thiland Heartthrob LaLisa:

  • 2009 - Lisa also participated in a singing contest as a school representative for “Top 3 Good Morals of Thailand “, where she finished as a runner-up.
  • 2010 - Lisa auditioned to join South Korean record label YG Entertainment in Thailand. Among 4,000 applicants, she was the only individual to qualify, which prompted the CEO to offer Lisa a chance to become a YG trainee.
  • 2013 - Lisa appeared in Taeyang's music video for the single “Ringa Linga, where she was one of the background dancers, alongside members of iKon and Winner.
  • 2015 - Lisa undertook her first modeling job for street-wear brand Nona9on.
  • 2016 - Lisa debuted as one of the four members of South Korean group Blackpink.
  • 2018 - Lisa unveiled her YouTube channel, Lilifilm Official, focusing on traveling and lifestyle alongside dance performance.
  • 2020 - Lisa served as the dance mentor throughout the iQIYI's Chinese girl group survival program Youth With You Season 2.
  • 2021 - Lisa returned as the dance mentor in the Chinese boy group survival show Youth With You Season 3.
  • 2022 - Lisa's debut single album Lalisa and its lead single were released. Upon release, she broke many world records.
